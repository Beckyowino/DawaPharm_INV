About Us

Dawa Pharmacy offers a wide range of medical supplies and medications, with knowledgeable staff available to help customers find what they need. Visit us today for high-quality products and excellent service.

How We Started

Dawa Pharmacy was founded in January 2023 in Nairobi's Umoja Estate with a mission to provide high-quality medical supplies and medications to the local community. Despite being a small operation, we quickly gained a reputation for our knowledgeable staff and commitment to customer service. In the beginning, we served just a handful of clients each day. But as word spread about our selection and service, our customer base grew rapidly. Today, we are proud to serve almost 70 clients a day, and we are constantly expanding our inventory to meet their needs.

We Are Trusted Company

Our team is made up of experienced pharmacists and support staff who are passionate about what they do. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality medical care, and we are dedicated to providing it at an affordable price. As we look to the future, we are excited to continue growing and serving the community. We are committed to staying up-to-date on the latest medical research and treatments, and to providing our clients with the best possible care. Thank you for choosing Dawa Pharmacy.
